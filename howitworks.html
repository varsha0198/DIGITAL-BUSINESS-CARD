How it works?

Create your digital Bussiness card

3 simple steps and you've got your very own digital business card:


Create your account fill the details and create your account.

Choose a Design choose between our selections of beautiful designs. All our templates are mobile and user friendly, look great and are easy to customize. Not sure what to choose? No worries! You can easily switch designs at any time.

Add your Content besides your contact details and social networks, consider adding a gallery of photos, Youtube videos and custom links so your customers get a rich experience of you!


Save to your device once you're done setting up your Digital Bussiness card save to your device.


Share whenever you want to share your bussiness card with someone, just open your digital bussiness card and click on the "Share" button on your card. You will then be able to choose from any of the sharing methods available (SMS, WhatsApp, Email and Facebook).
